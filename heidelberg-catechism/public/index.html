The Heidelberg Catechism (1563)

Question 1: What is your only comfort in life and in death?

That I am not my own, but belong— body and soul, in life and in death—to my faithful Savior, Jesus Christ.
(1 Cor.6:19-20, Rom.14:7-9)

Question 2: What must you know to live and die in the joy of this comfort?

Three things: first, how great my sin and misery are;
second, how I am set free from all my sins and misery;
third, how I am to thank God for such deliverance.
(Rom.3:9-10, 1 John 1:10)

Question 3: From where do you know your sins and misery?

from the law of God.
(Rom 3:20; 7:7-25)
